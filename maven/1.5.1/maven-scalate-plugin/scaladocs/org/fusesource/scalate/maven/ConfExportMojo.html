ConfExportMojo

class ConfExportMojo extends AbstractMojo

This goal exports confluence mark-up out of a Confluence wiki and adds the files to the resource target for Scalate to use in generating the site. Its guts are copied from the ConfluenceExport command. This should be made more modular.
